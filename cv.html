About

With my work experience on both international and local projects in the television and logistical sector, I bring a hands-on and positive work mentality to every task I take on.
Television productions such as the Olympic Games, Eurovision Song Contest, and multiple productions in the Middle East have provided me with high resistance for stress and a big amount of flexibility.

In the past years, I have graduated from a web developer course and followed other courses such as web game development to combine my passion for web technologies and creativity. As the market is continually evolving, so am I evolving by exploring new tools and searching for new courses to learn from others. At the moment I am following a graduate degree in programming to expand my developer skills in C# and other languages.

Currently, I am working on a web author project at Telenet Business, where I work with Adobe Experience Manager and Selligent.
